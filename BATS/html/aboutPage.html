BATS

Biosignal Analysis of (Poly)Trauma System

The purpose of this project is to provide a pipeline for biomedical data to be used by researchers for the study and diagnosis of polytrauma. Polytrauma is the combination of various physical and mental traumas in a person. We focused on the intersection of PTSD, depression, anxiety, post-concussive syndrome, and chronic pain. Our team will work towards creating an easy-to-use research tool focused on analyzing speech and neural data.
